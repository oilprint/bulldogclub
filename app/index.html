The club for you and your best friend

The Bulldog Club offers people an opportunity to meet like-minded people and bond with their dog.

About Us

The Bulldog Club of Kentucky is a non-profit organization whose members range from professional Bulldog breeders, people who compete at dog shows, owners of pet Bulldogs, and people who just love the breed.

Join our Team

When you join our club, you'll get an exciting year full of canine adventures, helping you to learn new skills, build confidence and make new friends through our programme of dog activities across the World.
